About Dr. Manika Khanna

Internationally Acclaimed Expert in Assisted Reproduction

Dr. Manika Khanna, MBBS, MD, D.A.G.E. (Germany), is an award-winning Infertility Specialist and Laparoscopic Surgeon who has been honored with numerous accolades for her exemplary achievements in the field of Assisted Reproduction.

Trained at Melbourne IVF, Australia, and the University of Kiel, Germany, she founded Gaudium IVF & Gynae Solutions Centre to realize her vision of bringing international standards of fertility care to India. The unprecedented success of the first Gaudium IVF Centre paved the way for Gaudium to evolve into the leading IVF clinic chain in India.

Hailed as the youngest consistent achiever in infertility specialist and IVF care, Dr. Manika has overseen thousands of successful IVF treatments, with healthy Gaudium babies growing in over 30 countries.

Redefining Your Fertility Journey with Gaudium IVF

At Gaudium IVF, we understand the profound desire to build a family. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to becoming a pioneer in the field of fertility care. For years, we've been at the forefront, providing world-class facilities, unparalleled expertise, and exceptional clinical outcomes. Our mission is to redefine the fertility experience. We want you to feel confident, knowing you have access to care and facilities comparable to the best global centers.

Here's what sets Gaudium IVF apart:

  • Super-Specialty Expertise: Our clinics are havens of advanced technology, staffed by leading fertility specialists, experienced embryologists, and a dedicated team focused on multidisciplinary care.
  • Cutting-Edge Technology: Experience the advantage of next-generation labs featuring INTEGRA Ti™, the world's most advanced system for ICSI procedures. We also utilize the latest equipment for egg quality evaluation, GE LOGIQ™ Ultrasound technology for precise monitoring, and state-of-the-art laminar flow OTs for a sterile environment.
  • Advanced Procedures: We offer cryopreservation using vitrification, a rapid-freezing technique that minimizes cell damage. Additionally, our Intracytoplasmic Morphologically-Selected Sperm Injection (IMSI) technology enhances ICSI outcomes through high-powered microscopic sperm selection.
  • Highly Technical USG-Guided Services: We are equipped to handle even complex cases. Our expertise includes hysterosalpingography (HSG), ultrasound-guided biopsies, transvaginal scans, follicular growth monitoring, transrectal ultrasounds, and ovarian cyst aspiration.

Patient-Centric Approach: At Gaudium IVF, you come first. We believe in quality consultations, confidentiality, and complete transparency throughout your journey.
